1. Turn off the water heater switch on outside unit
  2. Empty all 3 holding tanks and close them
  3. Open hot and cold low point drains
  4. Open all faucets
    1. Kitchen Sink
    2. Bathroom Sink
    3. Indoor Shower
    4. Outdoor Shower
    5. Hold down toilet flusher
    6. Let drain
  5. Close hot and cold low point drains
  6. Close all faucets
  7. Open valve to antifreeze pickup line (under couch)
  8. Put clear hose (antifreeze pickup line) into RV antifreeze
  9. Turn on pump
  10. Open one faucet at a time until pink antifreeze flows, then close
    1. Kitchen Sink
    2. Bathroom Sink
    3. Toilet
    4. Indoor Shower
    5. Outdoor Shower
  11. Turn off water pump
  12. Depressurize the system by opening a faucet for a few seconds
  13. Open low point drains (should see antifreeze come out)
  14. Close low point drains
  15. Push in on check valve for city water
    1. If you see antifreeze, then you are done
    2. Otherwise, have someone run the pump for 2 seconds while you're holding the checkvalve
  16. Close valve to antifreeze pickup line (under couch)
  17. Pour some antifreeze into drains
    1. Kitchen Sink (Both Sides)
    2. Bathroom Sink
    3. Toilet (flush down a little so all seals are covered)
    4. Indoor Shower
  18. Empty black and gray holding tanks
  19. YOU ARE DONE!
